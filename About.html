About Us

            If you're looking for a website to explore colleges, there are several good options available. Here are a few popular ones:

1. College Board's BigFuture: This website provides comprehensive information about colleges,
     including admission statistics, majors offered, and financial aid options.

2.   Naviance: Often used by high schools, Naviance offers tools for career exploration, 
     college planning, and application management.

3. Niche:  Formerly known as College Prowler, Niche offers reviews and rankings of colleges, 
    as well as insights into campus life and academics.

4. Princeton Review:  This site provides rankings, admissions advice, 
     and student reviews to help you make informed decisions about colleges.

5. U.S. News & World Report:  Known for its annual rankings of colleges and universities, 
     this site also offers detailed profiles and data on schools across the U.S.

6. College Confidential: A forum-based website where prospective students can ask questions, 
     read reviews, and get advice from current college students and alumni.

Each of these websites offers different features and perspectives, so it's a good idea to explore a few of them to get a well-rounded view of the 
colleges you're interested in.
